FreqExam

online hearing test

This application enables you to evaluate your hearing in terms of frequency range.

Instructions: Use headphones for best results. Click the button below to activate the app. Use the same button to toggle the sound on and off. Adjust volume and frequency using range sliders. Find your lower and upper hearing threshold, and select them with buttons below. Once you are done, type in your name and generate a personal report.

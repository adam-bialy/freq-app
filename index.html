Hearing frequency range test

This application enables you to assess your hearing range. Click the button below to activate the app. After that you can use the same button to toggle the sound on or off. Use sliders to adjust the volume and frequency of the sound. Use buttons below the slider to fetch your threshold values. Once you are done, type in your name and generate a personal report. We recommend using headphones.
